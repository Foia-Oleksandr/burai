BURAI-Release-Package (Ver.1.3.3)
---------------------------------

This is BURAI-Release-Package, which contains
 - BURAI 1.3.3 <https://github.com/BURAI-team/burai>
 - BURAI-tools <https://github.com/BURAI-team/burai-tools> .

[ FEATURE OF BURAI 1.3.3 ]
 - Add compatability with modern systems including Apple Silicon.

[ FEATURE OF BURAI-tools ]
 - Following tools are included:
   1. Executable files of QE
   2. Executable files of MPI
   3. Example files
   4. Pseudopotential files
 - License of BURAI-tools is GNU-GPLv3 .
